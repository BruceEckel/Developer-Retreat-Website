The Elm Retreat, October 6-9

Published: Thu 08 September 2016
By Bruce Eckel

In The Elm Retreat.

During the previous developer retreat (we worked on my Java 8 book), we decided we wanted to do another one where we try to plow through and figure out enough of the Elm language to start building the UI for the Open-Spaces Board Project (so Open-Spaces Conferences can have their boards in the cloud, rather than forcing a single physical location). We use Open Spaces during the Winter Tech Forum and similar events during the Summer.

This will be an open-source project hosted on Github.

The Elm retreat will be held October 6-9 in Crested Butte, Colorado.

We are assuming a background in programming but no prior experience with Elm. Some of us have already studied the language but in general we'll be learning it on the fly.

Developer retreats are still in the formative stages so there will be no fee, but you do need to arrange lodging and pay for meals. You can find out about travel and lodging here.
